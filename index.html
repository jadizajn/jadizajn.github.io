About Me

Hello! I'm a full stack designer and a digital enthusiast with great interest in AI.

I graduated University Collage Agora and won the Deans award. I was a design intern at BBDO Zagreb.

After graduating I continued my career at University Collage Agora as an assistant lecturer and a project manager / Creative lead on a project of creating the new visual identity and a website for Agora. After UCA I worked as a Creative lead for digital in Erste&Steiermärkische Bank d.d. on 3 award winning campaigns and many more projects. Apart from that I worked on many side projects of various scope.

I love technology and data driven user oriented design. Great design that is functional and generates a compeling experience and conversions. :) It is the reason why I wrote my thesis about The impact of AI and disruptive technologies on the creative industries and creative economy.

Capabilities

Brand Identity

Brand identity is not just a name, term, design or a symbol. It is the perfect combination of all of these based on information and measurable terms that makes you recognisable and allows you to connect to your costumers.

Brand Strategy

No matter how good your brand identity is structured, without a strategy and defining set of rules your brand can be literally invisible and all over the place. Brand strategy helps you grow your brand and defines it, especially today in the digital era.

Web Design

Web pages define your brand and it helps you establish credibility as a business. Most of the people look up businesses online, so leaving a good impression is what matters the most to a professional brand, especially if you want to establish an audience.

Video & Photography

The internet is a visual media and visual content defines whether your audience will stay on your page or not. Visual content and storytelling definee your brand personality and create engaging stories that drive people to engage with your content.

UI/UX Design

Good design is not just what looks good. It also needs to perform, convert, astonish, and fulfill its purpose. It has to be intuitive and easily udnerstod if not it may not perform well and won't get the job done.

Project managament

It’s about keeping your team focused on a goal and motivated to do their best to achieve it. It is about laying the groundwork for others’ success, and then standing back and letting them shine.
